SAFEGUARDING POLICY FOR CHILDREN'S HOMES

This policy outlines our commitment to safeguarding and promoting the welfare of all children in our care, in accordance with Ofsted requirements and statutory obligations.

1. INTRODUCTION
All children have the right to protection from harm. This policy ensures compliance with the Children Act 1989 and Care Standards Act 2000.

2. REPORTING PROCEDURES
Any concerns about a child's welfare must be reported immediately to the designated safeguarding officer. This includes physical harm, emotional abuse, sexual abuse, or missing incidents.

3. STAFF RESPONSIBILITIES
All staff members are required to:
- Complete annual safeguarding training
- Report concerns immediately
- Maintain professional boundaries
- Follow all policies and procedures

This policy is reviewed annually and updated in accordance with statutory guidance.
