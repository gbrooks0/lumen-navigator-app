OFSTED COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS

This document outlines the key Ofsted requirements for children's homes to ensure regulatory compliance.

1. REGISTRATION REQUIREMENTS
All children's homes must be registered with Ofsted and comply with the Care Standards Act 2000.

2. INSPECTION FRAMEWORK
Ofsted conducts regular inspections focusing on:
- Effectiveness of leaders and managers
- Quality of care and support
- Safeguarding children and young people
- Overall experiences and progress of children

3. REGULATORY STANDARDS
Homes must meet all regulatory requirements including:
- Suitable staff recruitment and training
- Health and safety procedures
- Educational provision
- Record keeping and notifications
