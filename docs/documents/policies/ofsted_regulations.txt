OFSTED REGULATIONS AND STATUTORY REQUIREMENTS

This document contains Ofsted regulations and statutory requirements for children's homes compliance.

REGULATION 1: REGISTRATION REQUIREMENTS
All children's homes must be registered with Ofsted under the Care Standards Act 2000.
This is a statutory requirement with legal obligations.

REGULATION 2: INSPECTION COMPLIANCE  
Ofsted inspections must assess compliance with all regulations and statutory guidance.
Non-compliance may result in regulatory action.

REGULATION 3: SAFEGUARDING STANDARDS
All safeguarding policies must comply with statutory safeguarding guidance and Ofsted requirements.
This includes compliance with government legislation and local authority safeguarding procedures.

REGULATION 4: REPORTING OBLIGATIONS
Statutory notifications must be submitted to Ofsted as required by regulation.
