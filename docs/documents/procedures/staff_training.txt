STAFF TRAINING PROCEDURES

This document outlines the training procedures and requirements for all staff members in children's homes.

1. INDUCTION TRAINING
New staff must complete a comprehensive induction program covering:
- Child development principles
- Communication techniques
- House rules and expectations
- Emergency procedures

2. ONGOING PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
All staff are required to:
- Attend monthly team meetings
- Complete annual refresher training
- Participate in supervision sessions
- Maintain professional development records

3. SPECIALIZED TRAINING
Staff working with specific needs must complete additional training in:
- Therapeutic interventions
- Behavioral management
- Educational support techniques
- Mental health awareness
